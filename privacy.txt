Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01-10-2023

Vivid School is committed to safeguarding the privacy of its students, parents, and website users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information of children aged 5 and above, as well as their families and guardians, in accordance with Tanzanian laws and international data protection standards.

1. Information We Collect
We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information: This includes names, addresses, contact numbers, email addresses, date of birth, health records (if necessary), and emergency contact details of students and guardians.
Academic Information: Data related to the student’s performance, attendance, and progress in school.
Online Activity: When you visit our website or use our digital platforms, we may collect information like IP addresses, browser types, and cookies to enhance the user experience.
2. How We Use the Information
We use the information we collect to:

Provide educational services and monitor students' progress.
Communicate with parents or guardians regarding the student’s progress, school events, or emergencies.
Maintain school records as required by Tanzanian law.
Improve our website and other digital platforms for a better user experience.
3. Data Protection and Security
We take reasonable steps to protect the personal data we collect, including:

Secure servers to store sensitive information.
Encryption of personal information, where applicable.
Restricted access to personal data, limited to authorized personnel.
4. Sharing Information with Third Parties
We will not sell or share personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

With the consent of parents or guardians.
To comply with legal obligations or in response to lawful requests by government agencies.
To authorized third-party service providers for the purpose of managing school-related services (e.g., health services, educational tools).
5. Parental Consent
For students under the age of 18, personal information is collected and used with the consent of a parent or legal guardian. We require parental consent for:

Participation in school activities that involve the sharing of personal data.
Access to our online learning platforms or any digital services.
6. Access and Correction of Personal Information
Parents or guardians have the right to:

Access the personal data we hold about their child.
Request corrections or updates to any inaccurate information.
To exercise these rights, you may contact us at [Insert Contact Information].

7. Retention of Personal Information
We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law.

8. Children’s Privacy
We understand the importance of protecting children's privacy, especially in an educational setting. Vivid School ensures that:

Personal information is collected only as necessary for educational purposes.
Children’s personal information is treated with the highest level of care and protection.
9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or Tanzanian law. Any significant changes will be communicated to parents and guardians through appropriate channels.
